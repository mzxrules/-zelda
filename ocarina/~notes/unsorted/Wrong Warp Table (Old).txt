The table works like this: The _To_ column represents the scene that is entered and where Farore's Wind will be placed, while the _From_ column lists where or how the scene was entered. _Exit_ represents the internal [entrance index](http://zeldaspeedruns.com/oot/wrongwarp/entrance-table) that will be stored in Farore's Wind. The _Deku Tree_, _Dodongo's Cavern_, and _Fire Temple_ columns list what occurs when wrong warping from the blue warps in these dungeons. 

Wrong warping may crash even if as listed as working depending on a number of factors:

- The entrance (not the coordinates) stored in Farore's Wind
- The scene you inevitably warp to
- The last cutscene played
- **Any** actions performed between watching the cutscene and performing the wrong warp. 

If a crash occurs, try watching a different cutscene before attempting the wrong warp. Many wrong warps work if with the title screen cutscene as the last cutscene.
